Louisiana Shrimp Gumbo (Serves 6)

Roux (5 Minutes - Low)

  • 1 cup any vegetable oil
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour (Gluten-Free Substitute)

Lousiana Holy Trinity (5 Minutes - Low)

  • 1 onion, peeled and chopped
  • 1 green pepper, seeds removed and chopped
  • 1 stalk celery, chopped

Aromatics (1 Minutes - Med)

  • 4 cloves garlic, peeled

Broth (5 Minutes - Med)

  • 2 cups chicken broth (Vegetarian Substitute)

Vegetables, Meat/Seafood, Herbs & Spices (30 Minutes - Med)

  • 3/4 cups ounces andouille, chorizo or Italian sausage, sliced (Vegetarian Substitute)
  • 1 cup shrimp, peeled and devined
  • 3 1/2 cups can whole tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup okra, fresh or frozen
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon sassafras (gumbo file powder)
  • 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper

Season & Taste (1 Minutes - Simmer)

  • 1 teaspoon sea salt

Garnish (1 Minutes - Off)

  • 2 green onions, thinly sliced
