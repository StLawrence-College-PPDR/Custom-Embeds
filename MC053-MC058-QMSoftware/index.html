Menu

The side menu is where the employee can access each of the different areas within the Quality Management Software. Employees can select to access training, documents, audits and inspections, non-compliance reports and corrective action reports, and any other areas an organization chooses to include.

Tasks

The Dashboard lets an employee know what the employee needs to do at work. In this example, the employee has some training to do, some documents to read, and audits and inspections to complete.

In-Progress

The Training area lets the employee know just how much of the training the employee has completed.

Past Due

The Past Due section of the Training area lets the employee know if there isn't any training that hasn't been completed within the given window. If an employee has any courses that are past due, those courses need to become a priority for the employee. There could be consequences for both the employee and the organization if training courses aren't completed on time.

Completed

The courses listed in the Completed section, of the training area, are a great resource for an employee. The employee can keep track of the completed courses to give proof of completion.

Documents

The documents section often contains multiple menus. Employees can create reports, depending on their authorization, view documents, and see how many documents they have read and created throughout a span of time.

State of Documents

The State of Documents section, of the Documents area, shows what documents are in an employee's process flow. If an employee is authorized, the employee may need to write and edit documents for the organization. The employee can see whether documents are in draft form, or have been reviewed, approved, or rejected. Employees can also see the total number of documents they are working on. Employees can select a document and continue to work on the document.

Document Library

Employees can find all of the important documents in the Document Library. SOPs, GMPs, and other important documents are housed with the document library. Employees can select the link and access the required file.

Document Pipeline

The Document Pipeline shows an employee how many documents the employee has filed within the past five months. The employee can also use the Document Pipeline to access documents they have created.

Audits

The audit area provides employees with a list of audits that are in process and complete. The author of the document and the date of the inspection are listed.

Inspections

The inspection area provides employees with a list of inspections that are in process and complete. The author of the document and the date of the inspection are listed.

Non-Conformity Reports

Employees can file failures, or non-conformities, in the QMS in this section. The employee can see how many NCRs the employee has initiated, and the status of any NCR initiated.

Corrective Action Reports

The audit area provides employees with a list of audits that are in process and complete. The author of the document and the date of the inspection are listed.
