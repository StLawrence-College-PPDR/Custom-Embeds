"When I ask contemporary artists why they've made VR pieces, some respond with disarming frankness: 'Because it's the future.' This is not a particularly profound reason, but it is undoubtedly significant. Even if we do not feel the potential of VR, we probably will come across it sooner or later. Thus, many digital media artists want to dive in head-first to explore and experiment with what is possible"

Joanne Popinska, Creative Director at Infinite Frame Media
