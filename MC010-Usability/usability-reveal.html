Characteristics of Usability

Learnability

Learnability

Learnability

This means that it is easy for new/first-time customers to use the service.

Efficiency

Efficiency

Efficiency

This means that the service can be engaged quickly with little effort.

Memorability

Memorability

Memorability

This means that if a user engages in a service a second time, even after an extended break since their first-time use, it is easy for them to re-learn.

Forgiveness

Forgiveness

Forgiveness

This means that there are low rates of errors in the way users engage the service, and that when they do make errors the system is forgiving to that.

Satisfaction

Satisfaction

Satisfaction

This means that users feel a degree of satisfaction during the process of using the service.
